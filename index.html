Staff Development


Staff development is a fundamental value at RFP and we therefore maintain internal and external training programmes. These take the form of internships, formal courses and informal job specific training covering various aspects of fixed fire protection ensuring our staff are competent and familiar with the relevant codes of practice. External training involves formal ASIB training, IFE courses and the funding of tertiary education at various institutions.

DESIGNS

Our customers are able to enjoy the benefits of our design office which is able to provide full design solutions, from site surveys, concept layouts, hydraulic calculations to shop drawings and fabrications schedules.

FABRICATION

Fabrication of our systems is done in house by fully equipped workshop manned by a complement of qualified boilermakers, fitters and welders drawing materials from our stores and using proven techniques and methodologies to industry standards.

INSTALLATION

We employ our installation teams to ensure we maintain and surpass industry standards for safety and quality to ensure customer expectations are delivered.

Accreditation

As a contractor that provides life safety installations, we are acutely aware of our responsibility to ensure that these meet the required industry standards. In our efforts to deliver on our mandate, we subscribe to the latest standards for fire protection and maintain close ties with the leading institutions.
